## Notes on Execution

- Target **800 to 1200 words** per article (longer for gift guides, shorter for card/message pieces).
- Reuse the existing article template exactly. Same CSS, same JSON-LD, same nav/footer.
- Every article must internally link to **at least 2 other articles** (mix of new and existing).
- Every article must end with a **CTA box** linking to `https://songsforyourstory.com/graduation.html#pricing` or the relevant occasion page.
- Every article must include **4-entry FAQ schema** in JSON-LD.
- Every article must have **og:image** (reuse `og-graduation.png` for graduation, `og-main.png` for general).
- Add each new URL to `sitemap.xml` on publish.
- Add each new article card to `articles/index.html` on publish.
- Voice: warm, conversational, specific. No em dashes. No AI slop words. Short paragraphs. 8th grade reading level.
