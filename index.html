Questions

Answers to what people usually ask.

How personalized are the songs?
Every song is 100% original, written from scratch based on the real names, stories, memories, and inside jokes you share with us. No templates. No generic lyrics. No two songs are ever the same.
How long does delivery take?
The Milestone delivers in 3 to 5 days. The Legacy delivers in 2 to 3 days. The Send-Off ships in 24 hours with priority service. You can also request a specific date if you need it by a particular event.
What occasions do you cover?
Any occasion. Birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, retirements, memorials, team anthems, business jingles, proposals, baby announcements, pet songs, apologies, inside jokes, and everything in between. If it's worth celebrating (or remembering), we can write a song for it.
What if I don't love the song?
Every order includes revisions. We'll keep refining the lyrics, tone, and production until it captures the story the way you imagined. If we've exhausted revisions and still haven't nailed it, we'll refund your order in full. No awkward emails.
Can I pick the genre and vocals?
Yes. You can request pop, country, hip-hop, R&B, acoustic, EDM, ballad, singer-songwriter, hype anthem, bilingual, or any other style. You can also specify male/female vocals, tempo, and overall vibe. If you're not sure, we'll recommend a direction based on the story.
Do I own the song?
Yes, you get full personal-use rights forever. Play it at events, share it with family, use it in slideshows or social posts. For commercial use (advertising, broadcasting, reselling), reach out and we'll work out an extended license.
