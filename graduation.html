Questions

Everything you'd want to know.

How does a custom graduation song actually work?
You share details about your graduate: their name, nickname, achievements, memories, inside jokes, future plans, and the vibe you want. We create a fully original, professionally produced song with real vocals and lyrics that reference all of those specific details. Delivered as a downloadable MP3.
How fast can I actually get the song?
The Send-Off package delivers in 24 hours. The Legacy in 2-3 days. The Milestone in 3-5 business days. We recommend ordering at least a week before graduation to allow time for revisions.
Why do the Legacy and Send-Off come with multiple songs?
Most families have more than one moment to celebrate. Multiple songs means you can do one for the graduate, one for a sibling, one for the whole friend group, or even one from mom + one from dad. Different angles, different stories, same graduate. Or split them across different graduates entirely. It's flexible.
Can I use it in a slideshow?
Yes, absolutely. You receive a high-quality MP3 that works perfectly for graduation slideshows, party playlists, or social media posts.
My graduate's ceremony is in 3 days. Can you still make it?
Yes. The Send-Off package delivers in 24 hours and The Legacy in 2-3 days. Even last-minute orders get the same radio-quality production.
What genres can you do?
Any genre. Pop, country, hip-hop, acoustic ballad, rock, R&B, Latin, folk, EDM, or anything else. We match the style to your graduate's personality and the moment.
Do I actually own the song?
Yes, 100%. The song is yours forever to keep, play at the graduation party, post on social, or treasure.
What if I hate it?
Every package includes revisions. We keep adjusting lyrics, tone, and production until it's right. If we've exhausted revision rounds and still can't nail it, we'll refund your order in full. We stand behind every song.
What info do I need to give you?
At minimum: your graduate's name, what school they're graduating from, and the vibe you want. The more details you share (nicknames, achievements, funny memories, where they're headed next), the more incredible the song. You'll fill out the full intake form right after checkout.
